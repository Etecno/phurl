Website Blacklists
These optional blacklists are here to prevent your URL shortener from spam. The more you check, the less likely you will be to recieve spam links. Spam links may be malicious and cause your URL shortener to be added to a blacklist itself, so it is important to keep at least one enabled.
Please tick the URL Blacklists you would like to use. The setting is the recommended choice from the developers.
